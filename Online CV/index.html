ABOUT ME

A former teacher turned entrepreneur, I find myself comfortably in the role as student and instructor. Building a company takes a lot of individual hard work but often overlooks the fact it doesn't just take one person, it takes a team. I'm a person that loves learning new things and also teaching others.

Originally from Columbus, Ohio, however since graduating The Ohio State University, I've lived and worked in 4 countries. My experience working in different environments has allowed me to strengthen my interdisciplinary skills and cultural awareness.

I worked primarily as a English teacher and school manager while in Taiwan, China and Mexico. When I arrived in Cape Town, South Africa, in 2016, I started my own company TEFL Recruits recruiting other teachers to teach around the world. We work with schools in over 20 different countries, and I manage remote recruitment teams in 3 different countries. I'm currently also learning to become a full-stack developer as I see exponential opportunities in the tech sector.

During my free time, I enjoy playing with my kids, Julian and Jackson. I enjoy going to the beach, hiking and pretty much anything that lets me be outside and enjoy nature.
